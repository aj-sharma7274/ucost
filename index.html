Climate Resilence Disaster Management

Emotional Resilience of Women, Children, and Other Vulnerable Sections Climate disasters disproportionately impact vulnerable groups, including women, children, and marginalized communities. This session will focus on emotional resilience, psychosocial support, and capacity-building measures to ensure inclusive recovery and resilience frameworks.

The Himalayan region, often referred to as the "water tower" of Asia, is home to diverse ecosystems, rich cultural heritage, and millions of livelihoods. Uttarakhand, nestled in the heart of this region, faces unique challenges due to climate change and its consequent impacts. Increasing frequency of extreme weather events, glacial melting, landslides, and floods highlight the urgent need for comprehensive climate resilience and disaster management strategies

About Us

The Uttarakhand State Council for Science and Technology (UCOST) is a leading institution dedicated to advancing science, technology, and innovation in Uttarakhand. UCOST plays a pivotal role in promoting research, capacity building, and community engagement to address the region's unique challenges. By fostering collaborations across government, academia, and industry, UCOST is at the forefront of initiatives aimed at sustainable development and resilience building in the Himalayan region.
